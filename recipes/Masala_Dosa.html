Masala Dosa

About this dish

Masala Dosa is readily available on street hawkers' carts anywhere in south India. But that is just Masala Dosa, not Mysore Masala Dosa. And they use the same Batter for all the Dosas. Also, for the Masala Dosa, they just put some Potato Curry in the centre and roast it well.

Ingredients Required

  • 3 cup sona masuri rice
  • ½ tsp methi / fenugreek seeds
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 2 tbsp toor dal
  • 2 tbsp chana dal
  • 1 cup poha / avalakki, rinsed

Steps

  1. Firstly, in a large bowl take 3 cup sona masuri rice and ½ tsp methi.Rinse well and soak in enough water for 4 hours.
  2. In another bowl take 1 cup urad dal, 2 tbsp toor dal and 2 tbsp chana dal.Rinse well and soak in enough water for 2 hours.
  3. After soaking dal for 2 hours, drain off the water and transfer to the grinder. You can also grind in mixi if you do not have access to a grinder and add water as required and blend to smoot
  4. Scrape sides. the smooth and fluffy batter will be ready after 40 minutes.Transfer the batter to a large vessel and keep aside.
  5. In the same grinder add soaked rice and 1 cup rinsed poha.Add water slowly and scrape the sides. blend to a coarse paste.
  6. Transfer the rice batter to the same urad dal batter .Mix well making sure everything is well combined.
  7. Ferment in a warm place for at least 8 hours or until the batter doubles in volume.
  8. Once the batter is well fermented, mix gently, without disturbing the air pockets.
  9. Transfer 4 cups of fermented batter to a small bowl and add 1 tsp salt.
  10. Mix well until the salt is well combined. masala dosa batter is ready and keep aside.
  11. Add a ladleful of batter on hot tawa.spread as thin as possible making a crispy dosa.Take 1 tsp of butter and spread uniformly.
  12. Also, place 2 tbsp of prepared aloo masala in the centre.Roast until the dosa turns golden brown and crisp and scrape the sides of dosa and roll the dosa.
  13. Finally, masala dosa recipe is ready to serve with coconut chutney and sambar.
